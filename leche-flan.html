Leche Flan Recipe

Leche Flan is a rich, creamy caramel custard that is a favorite Filipino dessert, especially in Pampanga.

Ingredients:

  • 10 large egg yolks
  • 1 can (395g) condensed milk
  • 1 can (370ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramelizing)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, melt sugar over medium heat until golden brown and liquid. Pour into a mold to coat the bottom.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Strain the mixture to remove any lumps, then pour over the caramelized sugar in the mold.
  4. Cover the mold with aluminum foil and steam for 30-40 minutes until set.
  5. Let it cool completely before flipping it onto a plate to serve.
