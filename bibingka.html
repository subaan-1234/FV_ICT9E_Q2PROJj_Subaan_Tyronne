Bibingka Recipe

Bibingka is a traditional Filipino rice cake often served during the Christmas season. It is soft, moist, and incredibly yummy

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Grated cheese and salted egg for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Gradually add coconut milk and melted butter until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into banana leaf-lined molds.
  5. Top with grated cheese and salted egg.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
