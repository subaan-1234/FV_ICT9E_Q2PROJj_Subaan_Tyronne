Pancit Luglug Recipe

Pancit Luglug is a Kapampangan noodle dish topped with a rich shrimp sauce and various flavorful garnishes.

Ingredients:

  • 400g rice noodles
  • 2 cups shrimp stock
  • 1/2 cup shrimp sauce
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crushed chicharon
  • 2 tbsp garlic, fried
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat shrimp stock and mix in shrimp sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour the sauce over the noodles and garnish with eggs, chicharon, and fried garlic.
  4. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.
