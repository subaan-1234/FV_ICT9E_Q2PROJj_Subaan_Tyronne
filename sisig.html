Sisig Recipe

Sisig is a sizzling pork dish that originated in Pampanga. This recipe balances the perfect mix of savory and tangy flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly
  • 1 pork liver, finely chopped
  • 3 red chilies, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp calamansi juice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Grill the pork belly until cooked and crispy. Chop into small pieces.
  2. In a pan, saut√© onions and chilies, then add chopped liver and pork belly.
  3. Mix in soy sauce and calamansi juice. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve on a sizzling plate and enjoy hot!
